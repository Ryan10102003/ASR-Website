About ASR

Your Dedicated Chemical Procurement Partner in Palghar

ASR Chemical Procurement Solutions is a sole-proprietorship firm founded to bridge the gap between industrial chemical manufacturers in the Tarapur–Boisar MIDC corridor and buyers across Maharashtra and Gujarat.

We specialise in sourcing, verifying, and facilitating the procurement of water treatment chemicals, solvents, pharma intermediates, and specialty chemicals — providing buyers with COA-verified products and competitive pricing through our extensive supplier network.

Our commission-based model means you pay nothing upfront. We earn only when you get exactly what you need, ensuring our goals are fully aligned with yours.

How We Work

Simple. Transparent. Results-Driven.

You tell us what you need, we do the sourcing. No advance fee — we earn only when you receive verified product.

1
Share Your Requirements

Tell us the chemical name, grade, quantity, delivery location, and target price. WhatsApp, call, or fill the form — whichever works best for you.

2
We Source & Verify

We identify MIDC-registered suppliers with confirmed stock, negotiate competitive pricing, and obtain the Certificate of Analysis (COA) for your review.

3
Deal & Delivery

Once you approve, we facilitate the transaction. Our commission is included transparently in the final quote. You deal directly with a verified source.
